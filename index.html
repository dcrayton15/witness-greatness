The Journey of LeBron James

Milestones Throughtout Lebron James Career

2003

NBA Debut

LeBron James was drafted #1 overall by the Cleveland Cavaliers.

2004

First NBA All-Star Appearance

LeBron made his first NBA All-Star appearance in just his second season.

2008

First NBA Scoring Title

LeBron won his first scoring title, averaging 30.0 points per game.

5,000 Points

LeBron reached 5,000 career points, becoming one of the youngest players to do so.

2009

First MVP Award

LeBron won his first NBA MVP award while leading the Cavaliers to 66 wins.

2012

First NBA Championship

LeBron wins his first NBA title with the Miami Heat.

2013

10,000 Points

LeBron surpassed 10,000 career points during his time with the Miami Heat.

2016

Cleveland Championship

LeBron leads the Cavaliers to their first NBA Championship.

2018

15,000 Points

LeBron continued his dominance, hitting the 15,000-point milestone.

2020

20,000 Points

LeBron scored his 20,000th point, further solidifying his place among the all-time greats.

Lakers Championship

LeBron wins his fourth NBA title with the Lakers.

2023

30,000 Points

One of the few players to ever reach 30,000 career points, LeBron added another historic moment to his career.

2023

NBA's All-Time Leading Scorer

LeBron James officially became the NBA’s all-time leading scorer, surpassing Kareem Abdul-Jabbar’s legendary record.
